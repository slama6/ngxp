About us

NextGen XP - Gaming Conference for Developers and Enthusiasts

NGXP is a gaming conference for developers, game fans, comic lovers, and technology enthusiasts. Want to start making games? Looking for inspiration, motivation, or the right people? You're in the right place!
The NGXP conference takes place twice a year as part of the international pop culture festivals AnimeShow / Comics Salon. These festivals have a long tradition and offer a rich gaming program. Game Salon has been a part of it from the beginning, but with the collaboration of SECTOR.sk, we've taken the section to the next level.

At the NextGen XP conference, you can look forward to:

  • Exclusive talks on game development and the gaming industry, including international speakers
  • Saturday developer meetup for networking
  • Insights from professionals directly from the gaming business
  • Indie Zone - a space for creators to showcase and test their games
  • Discussions with developers after every talk
  • A chance to win prizes after each lecture
